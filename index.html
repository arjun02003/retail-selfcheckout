Customer Journey

How It Works

From store entry to verified exit — in minutes, not queues.

1
Enter Store & Open App
Customer launches ScanGo, logs in, and selects their current store location from a verified list.
2
Scan Product Barcodes
Point camera at any product barcode. Product details and price appear instantly. Validated against store database to prevent barcode manipulation.
3
Manage Your Cart
Add quantities, remove items, apply coupons, and watch the running total update in real time. Out-of-stock items are blocked automatically.
4
Pay Digitally
Choose UPI, Credit/Debit Card, or Wallet. Payment is verified server-side — no fake screenshots accepted. Failed payments are handled with retry logic.
5
Receive Digital Receipt
A tamper-proof digital invoice with a unique transaction ID is generated. A time-limited exit QR code is issued — expires to prevent replay attacks.
6
Verified Exit
Security staff scans the exit QR. System validates against server in real time. Random basket inspections may be triggered. Customer exits — queue-free.

Anti-Theft Architecture

Security-First Design

01

Server-Side Payment Verification

No client-side payment confirmation. Every transaction is verified through the payment gateway API before any receipt is generated.

02

Time-Limited Exit QR

Exit QR codes expire after a configurable window (default 30 min). Prevents replay attacks and shared receipts.

03

Barcode Validation

All scanned barcodes are validated against the live store database. Unregistered barcodes are flagged and rejected.

04

Random Basket Inspection

Configurable random inspection rate triggers additional security checks at exit, reducing product swapping fraud.

05

Unique Transaction IDs

Every receipt carries a cryptographically unique transaction ID. Duplicate receipts are detected and rejected instantly.
